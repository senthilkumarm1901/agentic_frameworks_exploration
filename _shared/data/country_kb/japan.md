# Japan

- Japan is an archipelago consisting of 6,852 islands in the Pacific Ocean.
- It has the oldest population in the world, with over 29% of citizens aged 65 or older.
- Japan's Shinkansen (bullet train) network has maintained a near-perfect safety record since 1964 with zero passenger fatalities due to derailment or collision.
- The country is located in the Pacific Ring of Fire and experiences approximately 1,500 earthquakes per year.
- Japan has the third-largest automobile manufacturing industry in the world, home to Toyota, Honda, Nissan, and Suzuki.
- Japanese cuisine (washoku) was designated a UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage in 2013.
- The country has a constitutional monarchy with the Emperor serving as a ceremonial figurehead; the Prime Minister holds executive power.
- Japan's literacy rate is nearly 99%, one of the highest in the world.
