# Brazil

- Brazil contains approximately 60% of the Amazon rainforest, the world's largest tropical forest and a critical carbon sink.
- It is the only Portuguese-speaking country in the Americas and the largest in South America by both area and population.
- Brazil has won the FIFA World Cup a record five times (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002).
- The country is the world's largest producer of coffee, sugarcane, and oranges.
- Brazil's ethanol program makes it a global leader in biofuel production, with most vehicles running on flex-fuel engines.
- The Amazon River, which flows through Brazil, carries more water than any other river system on Earth.
- Carnival in Rio de Janeiro is the world's largest festival, attracting over 2 million people per day during the celebrations.
- Brazil has the most biodiversity of any country, with more species of plants, freshwater fish, and mammals than any other nation.
