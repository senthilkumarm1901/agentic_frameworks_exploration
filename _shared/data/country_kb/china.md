# China

- China is the world's most populous country and operates under a single-party socialist republic led by the Communist Party.
- The Great Wall of China is the longest human-made structure ever built, stretching over 13,000 miles across northern China.
- China has the world's oldest continuous civilization, with written records dating back over 3,500 years.
- Mandarin Chinese is the most spoken native language in the world, with multiple regional dialects across the country.
- The Three Gorges Dam on the Yangtze River is the world's largest hydroelectric power station by installed capacity.
- China invented paper, printing, gunpowder, and the compass, known as the Four Great Inventions of ancient China.
- The Terracotta Army in Xi'an, discovered in 1974, contains over 8,000 life-sized clay soldiers guarding Emperor Qin Shi Huang's tomb.
- China has the world's largest high-speed rail network, connecting major cities across its vast territory.
