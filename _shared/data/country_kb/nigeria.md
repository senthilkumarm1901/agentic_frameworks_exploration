# Nigeria

- Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa and the seventh most populous in the world.
- Nollywood, Nigeria's film industry, is the second-largest by volume of films produced annually, behind only India's Bollywood.
- Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, with Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo being the three largest.
- The country is Africa's largest oil producer and a member of OPEC.
- Lagos, Nigeria's largest city, is one of the fastest-growing megacities in the world.
- Nigeria has the largest economy in Africa by GDP, surpassing South Africa in 2014 after a GDP rebasing exercise.
- The country has a young population, with a median age of approximately 18 years.
- Nigerian literature has gained global recognition, with authors like Chinua Achebe (Things Fall Apart) and Wole Soyinka (first African Nobel Laureate in Literature, 1986).
