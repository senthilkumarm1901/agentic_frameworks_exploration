# United States - Culture and History

- It is home to Silicon Valley, the global center of technology innovation and venture capital.
- The country has the most Nobel Prize laureates of any nation, with over 400 recipients across all categories.
- The US National Park System protects over 85 million acres across 63 national parks.
- Hollywood, based in Los Angeles, produces the majority of the world's highest-grossing films.
- The US interstate highway system spans over 48,000 miles, making it one of the largest road networks in the world.
