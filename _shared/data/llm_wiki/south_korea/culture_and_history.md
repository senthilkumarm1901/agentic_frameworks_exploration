# South Korea - Culture and History

- K-pop (Korean pop music) has become a global cultural phenomenon, with groups like BTS achieving worldwide commercial success.
- South Korea has the fastest average internet connection speed in the world and highest smartphone penetration rate.
- The Korean alphabet, Hangul, was invented in the 15th century by King Sejong the Great and is considered one of the most scientific writing systems.
- South Korea and North Korea remain technically at war, as the Korean War ended with an armistice in 1953, not a peace treaty.
- South Korean cinema gained global recognition when "Parasite" became the first non-English language film to win the Academy Award for Best Picture in 2020.
- South Korea has one of the highest rates of plastic surgery per capita in the world, with cosmetic procedures widely accepted culturally.
