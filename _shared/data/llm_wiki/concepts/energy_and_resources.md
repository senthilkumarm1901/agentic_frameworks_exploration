# Energy and Resources

- Brazil: The country is the world's largest producer of coffee, sugarcane, and oranges.
- Brazil: Brazil's ethanol program makes it a global leader in biofuel production, with most vehicles running on flex-fuel engines.
- China: The Three Gorges Dam on the Yangtze River is the world's largest hydroelectric power station by installed capacity.
- India: India is the world's largest producer of milk and the second-largest producer of fruits and vegetables.
- Nigeria: The country is Africa's largest oil producer and a member of OPEC.
- Russia: Russia has the largest proven natural gas reserves in the world and is a leading exporter of energy resources.
- Saudi Arabia: Saudi Arabia is the world's largest exporter of petroleum and holds some of the largest proven oil reserves globally.
- South Africa: South Africa is the world's largest producer of platinum and chromium, and holds significant gold reserves.
