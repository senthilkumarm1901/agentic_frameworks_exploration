# Japan - Governance and Economy

- Japan's Shinkansen (bullet train) network has maintained a near-perfect safety record since 1964 with zero passenger fatalities due to derailment or collision.
- Japan has the third-largest automobile manufacturing industry in the world, home to Toyota, Honda, Nissan, and Suzuki.
- The country has a constitutional monarchy with the Emperor serving as a ceremonial figurehead; the Prime Minister holds executive power.
