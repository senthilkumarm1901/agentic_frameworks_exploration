# Germany - Culture and History

- The country is known as the birthplace of the Protestant Reformation, initiated by Martin Luther in 1517.
- Germany has produced more classical music composers than any other country, including Bach, Beethoven, Brahms, and Wagner.
- Germany is the birthplace of the modern printing press, invented by Johannes Gutenberg around 1440.
- The country has more zoo facilities than any other nation in the world, with over 400 registered zoos and animal parks.
- German is the most widely spoken native language in the European Union.
- The Berlin Wall, which divided East and West Berlin from 1961 to 1989, became the most prominent symbol of the Cold War division.
