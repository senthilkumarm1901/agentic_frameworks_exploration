# France - Culture and History

- France is the most visited country in the world, with Paris being the most popular city destination globally.
- The Académie française, founded in 1635, is the official authority on the French language and its usage.
- French cuisine was inscribed on UNESCO's Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity in 2010.
- The Tour de France, established in 1903, is the world's most famous and prestigious bicycle race.
- France has won more Nobel Prizes in Literature than any other country, with authors like Albert Camus and Jean-Paul Sartre among its laureates.
