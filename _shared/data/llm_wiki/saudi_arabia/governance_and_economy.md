# Saudi Arabia - Governance and Economy

- The country operates as an absolute monarchy, with the King serving as both head of state and head of government.
- Saudi Arabia is the world's largest exporter of petroleum and holds some of the largest proven oil reserves globally.
- The country follows Sharia law, with Islamic principles forming the basis of its legal and judicial system.
