# India - Culture and History

- The country has 22 officially recognized languages and over 19,500 dialects spoken across its states.
- India's space agency ISRO became the first to successfully orbit Mars on its maiden attempt (Mangalyaan, 2014).
- The Indian Railways is one of the largest employers in the world, with over 1.2 million employees.
- India is the world's largest producer of milk and the second-largest producer of fruits and vegetables.
