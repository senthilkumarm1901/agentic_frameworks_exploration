# Canada - Geography and Environment

- Canada has the longest coastline of any country in the world, bordered by three oceans: the Atlantic, Pacific, and Arctic.
- Canada contains approximately 20% of the world's freshwater, including the Great Lakes shared with the United States.
- The country has the longest undefended border in the world, sharing it with the United States.
