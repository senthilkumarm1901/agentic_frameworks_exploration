# Russia - Culture and History

- The Russian language uses the Cyrillic alphabet, which was developed in the First Bulgarian Empire during the 9th century.
- Russia has produced renowned literary figures including Tolstoy, Dostoevsky, Chekhov, and Pushkin, shaping world literature.
- The country's classical ballet tradition, with companies like the Bolshoi and Mariinsky, is considered among the finest in the world.
- Russia was the first nation to send a human into space when Yuri Gagarin orbited Earth in 1961.
