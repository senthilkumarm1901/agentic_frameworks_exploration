# Spain - Culture and History

- The Spanish language originated in the Castile region and is now the world's fourth most spoken language by total speakers.
- The Alhambra in Granada is one of the finest examples of Moorish architecture, reflecting Spain's Islamic heritage from 711-1492 CE.
- The Running of the Bulls (Encierro) in Pamplona is one of the most famous and controversial traditional festivals in the world.
- Spanish artists including Picasso, Dalí, Goya, and Velázquez have made lasting contributions to Western art history.
