# Egypt - Geography and Environment

- The Nile River, flowing through Egypt, is the longest river in the world and the source of Egypt's agricultural civilization.
- The Suez Canal, opened in 1869, connects the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea and is one of the world's most heavily used shipping lanes.
- Egypt spans two continents, with the Sinai Peninsula located in Asia while the rest of the country is in Africa.
